AES 256-Bit Encryption

Instructions: Enter the message you would like to encrypt in the "Enter your message" box. Enter your encryption key (which can be any numbers, letters, or combinations you want) in the "Enter your encryption key" box. Then click "encrypt". To decrypt a message, input its encryption key, and enter the encrypted text in the "Encrypted Message" box. Then click "Decrypt."

Proper Formatting: The subject (title) of your email should be the encryption key. The body of the email should contain the encrypted text.
